History of Flat Design

Flat Design has evolved from earlier design principles into the popular aesthetic we see today. Here's a brief timeline:

1940s: Swiss Style

Dominates the scene with grids, sans-serif typography, and clean hierarchy. Heavily influenced by minimalism.

2006: Microsoft

Releases Zune media player - large, lowercase typography and background imagery.

2010: Microsoft

Releases Windows Phone 7 - large, bright grids, sans-serif, flat icons. Dubbed "Metro" design.

2012: Microsoft

Releases Windows 8 with Metro design - block grid layout, sharp edges, bright colours and large background images.

2013: Apple

Releases iOS 7 - flat design shakes off previous skeuomorphic approach. Skeuomorphism across the web quickly becomes outdated.
